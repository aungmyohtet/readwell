### Option B — Full graded launch

Launch only when:
- A2 is complete and polished
- B1 is complete and polished
- B2 has no major curriculum holes

Use this option if the public promise is true A2-B2 coverage.

## Editorial QA Rubric

Every chapter must pass all six checks before launch.

### 1. Grammar Focus

- the target structure appears naturally several times in the reading
- the target is visible enough to notice without making the prose artificial
- the grammar explanation is accurate, concise, and learner-facing
- the examples match the chapter language and the stated rule

### 2. CEFR Control

- sentence length fits the intended level
- abstractness and density match the level
- grammar is challenging in the intended way, not because the prose is muddy
- vocabulary load stays inside the level target

### 3. Vocabulary Support

- vocabulary items are useful, not decorative
- words appear naturally in the story body
- example sentences are clear and level-appropriate
- vocabulary supports the chapter theme and grammar focus

### 4. Reading Quality

- the text sounds like a believable graded reader, not an exercise sheet
- paragraph flow is smooth
- tone is age-appropriate
- dialogue and narration feel natural

### 5. Quiz Quality

- questions test real comprehension, not only sentence matching
- distractors are plausible
- explanations teach, not just justify the answer
- at least some items reinforce the grammar focus indirectly through meaning

### 6. Annotation Quality

- grammar annotations highlight meaningful whole forms, not fragments
- highlighted text matches the rule being taught
- notes are short and useful
- annotation density is enough to support noticing without clutter

## Go / No-Go Criteria

### Go for narrow launch when

- A2 is clean and stable
- selected B1 chapters pass the rubric
- the catalogue positioning clearly states the current scope
- no level included in marketing has major grammar holes

### Go for full launch when

- A2, B1, and B2 all have credible curriculum coverage
- every launch chapter passes the editorial rubric
- all launch files validate cleanly
- import and UI smoke tests pass on the full catalogue

### No-Go conditions

- B2 is marketed as available while units 5-12 are still missing
- a noticeable share of chapters still read like drafts
- comprehension quality is inconsistent across stories
- annotation quality varies enough to confuse learners
